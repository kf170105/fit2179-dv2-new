Malaysia faces a persistent challenge in maintaining
adequate blood supplies. Malaysia requires over 2,000
bags of blood daily, yet only 2.2% to 2.3% of the
population donates annually. This dashboard explores
patterns hidden in donation records from geographic
disparities to seasonal trends and donor loyalty to
understand how Malaysia can strengthen its blood
donation culture.
